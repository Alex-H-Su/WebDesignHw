Gluten Free Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups gluten free flour
  • 1 teaspoon xanthum gum only if your flour does not contain it
  • 1.5 cups brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 cup melted coconut oil or butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • love

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350'
  2. mix dry and wet ingredients seperately then combine
  3. mix more
  4. keep mixing
  5. ok thats good, now add the chocolate chips
  6. and yes, mix those in too
  7. scoop out 2 in balls of dough onto a sheet pan lined with parchment paper leaving 2 inches of space
  8. push down on each ball with a fork leaving a cross hatch because they are peanut butter cookies and that is just what you do
  9. stop eating the cookie dough, it has raw eggs and you may get sick
  10. bake for 11-13 minutes and put them on a rack to cool
  11. recipe makes 24 cookies unless you struggle with volume
